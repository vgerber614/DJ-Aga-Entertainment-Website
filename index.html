About


I grew up in Upstate New York and started DJ’ing while in high school during the late 80's. After high school, I joined the Navy and served for 24 years. After retiring from the Navy, I lived on the beautiful island of Oahu in Hawaii and started DJ’ing at parties and fundraisers. I was also able to DJ at a few events in the Philippines and Japan. It wasn’t until I moved to San Antonio that I started my mobile DJ venture in 2021. My goal as a mobile DJ is to bring the words "Affordable" and "DJ'ing" back together. I specialize in 70’s Disco, 80's to current Pop, R&B, Hip-Hop/Rap, Dance/EDM, Latino club hits and Miami Freestyle (Aka Latin Hip-Hop). I have also learned to play country music, various Latino genres such as Raggaeton and Cumbia along with Tejano. Weddings, 80's themed parties, Christmas and pool parties are my favorites!! I also enjoy providing DJ services for Quinceaneras, Sweet 16’s, birthday parties, graduations, retirements and fundraisers. I use high quality sound-systems and smart controlled lighting to bring the good vibe to the party. Contact me for questions or inquiries.
